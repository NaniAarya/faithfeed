About This Build

What Makes This Different

A quick, honest rundown of the choices behind this build — and the gap between "a Bible site" and this one.

The Signature Move

Every chapter change drops a bead of colour into the reading panel — it falls, ripples, and dissolves into the ambient blobs via an SVG "goo" filter, the same trick used for realistic liquid-merge effects.

Built for Real Data

The book/chapter structure is accurate to all 66 books. Swap the placeholder VERSES object for your parsed PDF text and every dropdown, card and animation keeps working — no rebuild needed.

Honest Placeholders

Rather than invent scripture text for 1,189 chapters, only well-known passages are hand-set. The rest are clearly labelled placeholders — accuracy matters more than a fully "filled" demo.
